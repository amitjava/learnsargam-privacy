Privacy Policy for Learn Sargam

Last updated: December 26, 2025

Overview

Learn Sargam ("the App") is a music learning application designed to help users practice Indian classical music. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Microphone Access

The App requires microphone access to detect the pitch of your voice while you practice singing. This is essential for providing real-time feedback on your musical accuracy.

Important:
  • All audio processing happens locally on your device
  • We do not record, store, or transmit any audio data
  • No voice recordings are saved or sent to any server

Local Storage

The App stores your practice progress and settings locally on your device. This includes:

  • Your selected base frequency (Sa calibration)
  • Practice statistics and scores
  • App preferences

This data never leaves your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not record or store audio
  • We do not use analytics or tracking
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not require account creation

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
