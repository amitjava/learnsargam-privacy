Privacy Policy for Learn Sargam

Last updated: January 5, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Pari-Yuvi Games Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use the Learn Sargam mobile application ("the App").

Learn Sargam is a music learning application designed to help users practice Indian classical music through real-time pitch detection and feedback.

Developer Information

Company: Pari-Yuvi Games Inc.

Contact Email: privacy@learnsargam.app

App Name: Learn Sargam (com.learnsargam.app)

Information Collection and Use

Microphone Access

The App requires microphone access to detect the pitch of your voice while you practice singing. This permission is essential for providing real-time feedback on your musical accuracy.

Important - Audio Data Handling:
  • All audio processing happens locally on your device in real-time
  • We do NOT record, store, or transmit any audio data to external servers
  • No voice recordings are saved anywhere
  • Audio data is processed instantly and discarded immediately after pitch detection

Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your selected base frequency (Sa calibration)
  • Practice statistics and scores
  • App preferences and settings

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT collect:

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do NOT record or store audio/voice data
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do NOT require account creation or login
  • We do NOT collect precise location data
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other device data
  • We do NOT collect or store payment information (handled by Google Play)

Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

Google Play Services

The App uses Google Play Services for in-app purchases. When you make a purchase, Google processes the transaction. We do not collect or store your payment information (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.). All payment processing is handled securely by Google. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Purchase-related data we may receive from Google:

  • Purchase tokens to verify transactions
  • Product IDs of purchased items
  • Purchase status (active, expired, refunded)

This information is used solely to unlock premium features you have purchased.

Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks. These ad networks may collect and use certain data to show you personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable identifier for advertising)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • General location (country/region level, not precise location)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Adjusting your device's ad settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization)
  • Resetting your advertising ID

We do not share any personal information or audio data with advertising partners.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device lock features (PIN, fingerprint, face recognition)

Data Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the application

Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered as we do not maintain any copies on external servers.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and can be viewed within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete all app data by clearing the app data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app
  • Portability: Since we don't collect data on servers, there is no external data to export
  • Opt-out: You can revoke microphone permissions at any time through your device settings

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. Since the App does not collect personal data, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the legal basis for any minimal data processing is:

  • Legitimate Interest: Processing device-local data is necessary for the app's core functionality (pitch detection for music practice)
  • Consent: By granting microphone permission, you consent to real-time audio processing for pitch detection

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information. However, since Learn Sargam does not collect, sell, or share personal information, these provisions do not apply to our app. We do not sell personal information to third parties.
